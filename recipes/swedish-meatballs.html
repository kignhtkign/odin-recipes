Swedish Meatballs

Easy Slow Cooker Swedish Meatballs

This Swedish meatball crockpot recipe is a perfect make-ahead slow cooker family meal.

Serve over wide egg noodles. Meatballs can also be served without the noodles as an appetizer.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 tbsp. steak sauce
  • 1 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • ½ tsp. paprika
  • ½ tsp. salt
  • ½ tps. ground black pepper
  • 1 (28oz.) package frozen cooked meatballs, thawed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 cup sour cream

Steps:

  1. Whisk together beef broth and condensed soup in a slow cooker. Add steak sauce, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir in meatballs and onions.
  2. Cook on High for 4 hours.
  3. Stir in sour cream. Continue cooking until the sauce is heated through, about 30 minutes.
  4. Serve hot.
